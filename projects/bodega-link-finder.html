About BodegaLink Finder

Connecting suppliers and resellers across the Philippines

Our Mission

BodegaLink Finder was created to solve the challenges faced by small suppliers and resellers in the Philippines. Traditional supply chains are complex, inefficient, and often exclude small businesses. Our platform creates a direct connection between suppliers and resellers, eliminating unnecessary middlemen and reducing costs for everyone.

How We Help Suppliers

  • Reach thousands of potential customers nationwide
  • Showcase your products with detailed listings
  • Get discovered by resellers looking for your products
  • Build credibility with our verification system

How We Help Resellers

  • Find verified suppliers near you
  • Compare prices and product offerings
  • Connect directly with suppliers via your preferred channel
  • Access wholesale pricing without minimum order requirements

Our Team

Founded by entrepreneurs with experience in Philippine retail and e-commerce, our team is passionate about empowering small businesses. We understand the unique challenges of the Philippine market and have built a solution specifically for Filipino businesses.
